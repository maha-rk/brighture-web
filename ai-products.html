Product Portfolio

Five Systems.
One Safety Platform.

Each module works standalone or as part of a unified deployment — covering every dimension of warehouse safety intelligence.

01 — Core System

Real-time Hazard Detection System

Our primary AI engine continuously scans camera feeds to identify dangerous proximity events, falling objects, unauthorized zone access, and unsafe forklift behavior — the moment they occur.

  • Multi-camera real-time analysis at 30fps with sub-300ms latency
  • Detects 40+ hazard types including near-misses, falling loads, and overspeeding
  • Automatic zone-based alerting with configurable safety perimeters
  • Works in low-light, high-dust, and partially obstructed environments
02 — Worker Safety

Worker Safety Monitoring

Camera-based AI tracks PPE compliance, ergonomic postures, fatigue indicators, and proximity to moving machinery — ensuring every employee goes home safely.

  • Real-time PPE detection: hard hats, vests, gloves, and safety shoes
  • Fatigue and distraction monitoring using behavioral AI models
  • Ergonomic risk scoring with automated task-rotation suggestions
  • Lone worker alerts and man-down detection in dead zones
03 — Operations

Traffic & Flow Management

AI-powered traffic orchestration routes forklifts optimally, enforces speed limits automatically, predicts congestion before it forms, and maximizes throughput across every aisle.

  • Automated speed enforcement with vehicle ID tagging
  • Predictive route optimization reducing travel time by up to 28%
  • Real-time heatmapping of traffic density and bottleneck zones
  • Intelligent loading dock scheduling and queue management
04 — Prevention

Incident Prevention & Alert System

Predictive risk scoring identifies hazardous patterns before they escalate. Automated multi-channel alerts reach the right people instantly — every time.

  • Pre-incident risk score with up to 15-second advance warning
  • Automated alerts via SMS, email, PA system, and light signals
  • Escalation workflows: worker → supervisor → safety manager
  • Incident logging with timestamped video evidence clips
05 — Intelligence

Analytics & Reporting Dashboard

A unified command center giving safety managers, operations leaders, and executives complete facility visibility — from live feeds to long-term trend analysis and compliance reporting.

  • Real-time dashboard with drill-down by zone, shift, or vehicle
  • Automated regulatory compliance reports (OSHA, ISO 45001)
  • Predictive maintenance alerts for forklifts based on behavioral data
  • Benchmarking against industry safety standards and historical baselines
